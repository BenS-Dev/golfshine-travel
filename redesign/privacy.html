Privacy Policy

We treat your personal information the way we treat your travel plans: with care, discretion, and respect.

Effective date: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gulfshine Services Inc., doing business as Gulfshine Travel ("Gulfshine," "we," "us"), collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit gulfshineservices.us or contact us to plan a trip. We are a Florida travel agency located at 3100 NW 2nd Ave, Unit 204, Boca Raton, FL 33496 (Fla. Seller of Travel Ref. No. ST46553).

Information We Collect

We collect only the information you choose to share with us. When you submit our trip inquiry form, that includes your name, email address, optional phone number, destination interest, trip type, rough travel dates, and anything you write in your message. If you email or call us directly, we receive whatever information you provide in that conversation.

We do not require accounts, and we do not collect payment information through this website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you share to respond to your inquiry, prepare and refine trip proposals, arrange the travel services you request, and keep in touch with you about your plans. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Cookies and Analytics

This website does not set cookies, does not use analytics or advertising trackers, and does not build profiles of visitors. You can browse every page without being tracked.

Service Providers

A small number of trusted providers help us operate this website:

  • Resend delivers your inquiry form submission to our inbox as an email. Your form details pass through Resend's systems solely for that delivery.
  • Google Fonts supplies the typefaces on this site. When a page loads, your browser requests font files from Google's servers, which may log your IP address as part of serving the request.
  • Our web hosting provider serves the site and may keep standard server logs (such as IP address, pages requested, and timestamps) for security and reliability.

When you book a trip, we share only the details necessary with the airlines, hotels, golf clubs, guides, and other suppliers who deliver your travel arrangements.

How Long We Keep Information

We keep inquiry and booking correspondence for as long as needed to serve you and to meet legal, accounting, and record-keeping obligations that apply to Florida sellers of travel. When information is no longer needed, we delete it.

Your Choices and Rights

You may ask us at any time to see the personal information we hold about you, correct it, or delete it. Email contact@gulfshineservices.us and we will respond promptly. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under local privacy laws, and we will honor valid requests to exercise them.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for adults planning travel. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We limit access to your information to the people who need it to plan your trip, and we use reputable providers to store and transmit it. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect what you share with us.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will post the updated version on this page with a new effective date.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your information are always welcome.
