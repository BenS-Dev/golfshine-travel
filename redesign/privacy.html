Privacy Policy

We treat your personal information the way we treat your travel plans: with care, discretion, and respect.

Effective date: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gulfshine Services Inc., doing business as Gulfshine Travel ("we," "us"), handles personal information collected through gulfshineservices.us. We are a Florida travel agency located at 3100 NW 2nd Ave, Unit 204, Boca Raton, FL 33496 (Fla. Seller of Travel Ref. No. ST46553).

What We Collect

Only what you choose to share with us: the details you submit through our trip inquiry form (name, email, optional phone, and your trip preferences) or provide by email or phone. We do not require accounts and do not collect payment information through this website.

How We Use It

We use your information solely to respond to your inquiry and plan and arrange the travel you request. We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not set cookies and does not use analytics or advertising trackers.

Sharing

We share information only with the service providers that operate this website and deliver your inquiry to us, and with the travel suppliers needed to arrange the trip you request. Each receives only what is necessary for that purpose.

Your Choices

Email contact@gulfshineservices.us at any time to see, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and we will respond promptly.

Changes

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
