Punta Cana Golf Packages

Caribbean · Dominican Republic · Multi-course golf

Multiple Punta Cana stay & play packages across the region's top golf resorts. Courses include Corales Golf Club, Hard Rock Golf Club, Iberostar Bavaro and La Cana.

Iberostar Bavaro Package

  • 4 nights in Garden View Suite.
  • 3 rounds of golf with cart at Iberostar Bavaro, Corales, Punta Espada.
  • Booking bonus: $250 Callaway Gift Card.
  • Beverage cart drinks and snacks.
  • Transport to/from golf courses and PUJ Airport.

Westin Punta Cana - 3 Nights

Warm sunshine and refreshing sea breezes.

  • 3 Nights Traditional Ocean View Room.
  • 3 Rounds of golf with cart.
  • Daily $75 credit per person for premium beverages.
  • Roundtrip airport transfers with VIP greeting service.
  • Package does not include air travel.

Westin Punta Cana - 4 Nights

  • 4 Nights Traditional Ocean View Room.
  • 1 Round at La Cana Golf Club, 2 Rounds at Corales Golf Club.
  • Similar benefits structure to the 3-night package.

Hard Rock & Grand Bahia Principe

Enjoy an exciting golf and beach paradise getaway.

  • 3 Nights at Grand Bahia Principe Bavaro.
  • 3 Rounds with cart at Hard Rock Golf Club.
  • Includes daily resort fee and applicable package fees.
  • Extra nights available. Other courses may be added.
