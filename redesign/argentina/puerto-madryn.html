Puerto Madryn

Argentina · Choose a 3-night stay, or extend to 6 nights with Buenos Aires.

A focused 4-day stay on the Patagonian Atlantic coast.

The program includes the following services:

  • At Puerto Madryn: Lodging at the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Traditional Excursion to Peninsula de Valdez.

Day 1: Buenos Aires / Puerto Madryn

From the Airport of the City of Buenos Aires you will embark toward the Patagonia. You will step Puerto Madryn, area of Frank Whales and of frank winds. Transfer to the selected hotel. The rest of the day is free. According to your flight time, you will be able to take advantage to carry out some activity or trip. The Submarine Baptism would be a great experience of Diving in the Argentinean Sea. At night a walk for the coast will define the infinite on the sea.

Day 2: Puerto Madryn

Breakfast. Today you begin to palpitate the steppe patagónica… and their fauna. For that reason you will leave toward Peninsula of Valdes, knowing Puerto Pirámides. In this day we suggest you to add to the program the Avistaje of Whales, unique and surprising. In the afternoon the sun goes hiding in the plateau, while you return to Puerto Madryn. Lodging in the hotel.

Day 3: Puerto Madryn

Breakfast. The day is totally free, but that is not what we suggest you! There is a variety of trips to carry out like visiting Punta Tombo's Penguins, one of the most important reservations in the world. At night lodging in the hotel.

Day 4: Puerto Madryn / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. Transfer to the Puerto Madryn Airport to return to Buenos Aires. Arrive to Buenos Aires City.

Buenos Aires + Puerto Madryn - 3 nights each, with a city tour and Tango Show.

The program includes the following services:

  • At Buenos Aires: 3 nights of lodging in the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers to Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Tour along the Buenos Aires City.
  • At Puerto Madryn: 3 nights of lodging at the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Traditional Excursion to Peninsula de Valdez.
  • Dinner Tango's Show.

Day 1: Buenos Aires

Welcome to the Tango's city, we were waiting for you! Once you arrive the International Airport of Ezeiza, you will be taken to the chosen hotel. During the evening it will fulfil a City Tour to take the first contact with the take care. The rest of the day is free to begin feeling the demurrage. At night, lodging.

Day 2: Buenos Aires

Breakfast. It's time to enjoy Buenos Aires night life. Lodging.

Day 3: Buenos Aires / Puerto Madryn

Breakfast. Transfer to the Airport of Buenos Aires' City to fly to the Patagonia. You'll land there, in a place characteristic because off the Francas' Whales and winds. Transfer to the chosen hotel. The rest of the day is free. According to your flight time, you could do another activity or excursion. The Submarine Baptism could be an great experience about diving in the Argentine Sea. At night we suggest a walk along the coast side. Lodging.

Day 4: Puerto Madryn

Breakfast. Start beating the patagonic steppe and its fauna. That's why you'll depart to Valdes Peninsula, knowing Pirámides' Port. During that day we suggest you to add the Avistaje de Ballenas' program. Unique and amazing. By the afternoon the sun goes down trying to be hidden in the tableland, while you return to Madryn Port. Lodging at the hotel.

Day 5: Puerto Madryn

Breakfast. The day is completely free, but that is not what we suggest you! There are a lot of excursions that you can do like visiting the Punta Tombo's penguins, the most important source of the world. At night, lodging at the hotel.

Day 6: Puerto Madryn / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. Transfer to the Airportof Trelew / Madryn. Arrive at the Aiport of Buenos Aires's City. In the night Tango Show at Carlos Gardel Corner. Lodging at the chosen hotel.

Day 7: Buenos Aires

Breakfast. According to your flight time, you could still have a walk round Buenos Aires's City. Transfer to the Airport of Buenos Aires.
