Buenos Aires - 4 Days / 3 Nights

Argentina · 3-Night Program

The program includes the following services:

  • At Buenos Aires: Lodging in the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers to Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • City Tour.
  • Full day "The Gaucho's party".
  • Dinner "Tango show".

Day 1: Buenos Aires

Welcome to the Tango's city, we were waiting for you! Once you arrive the International Airport of Ezeiza, you will be taken to the chosen hotel. The rest of the day is free to begin feeling the demurrage. At night, lodging.

Day 2: Buenos Aires

The day starts with a good breakfast. During the morning it will take place a City Tour around the City. The rest of the day will be free to have full contact with Buenos Aires. We suggest you that at night you have a dinner with a Tango's Show at Carlos Gardel Corner. At night, lodging at the hotel.

Day 3: Buenos Aires

Breakfast. Full day excursion "Gaucho Party", including lunch tipic "Asado Criollo", folk music, gaucho skills. In the evening, we return to the city. It's time to enjoy Buenos Aires night life. Lodging.

Day 4: Buenos Aires

Breakfast. According to your flight time, you can still wall around Buenos Aires. Transfer to the Airport.
