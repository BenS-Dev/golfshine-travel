Buenos Aires, Iguazú, Puerto Madryn & El Calafate

Argentina · 15 Days / 13 Nights

The program includes the following services:

  • All destinations: Tranfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Buenos Aires: 4-night accommodation in the chosen hotel, breakfast included. A Tour in Buenos Aires City. Dinner Tango's Show.
  • Iguazu: 3-night accommodation in the chosen hotel, breakfast included. Excursion to Iguazu Falls visiting both the Argentinean, and the Brazilian side.
  • Puerto Madryn: 3 night accommodation in the chosen hotel with breakfast. Excursion to Punta Tombo with toninas sightseeing and Excursion to Peninsula Valdes with whales sightseeing.
  • El Calafate: 3-night accommodation in the chosen hotel, breakfast included. Excursion to Perito Moreno Glacier bridges.

Day 1: Europe / America

With the south cross as a guide you fly over the Atlantic Ocean to have an unforgettable experience in Argentina with its incredible scenery and wild nature.

Day 2: Marvellous Buenos Aires

The city tour lets you discover the great city located on the Rio de La Plata. Buenos Aires captures her visitors at first sight, specially its numerous cultural options.

Day 3: Free day in Buenos Aires

Near the city, you can find different lifestyles. On a special country day, you can be indulged with famous "Asado Criollo" and a typical horse show as a part of the "Fiesta Gaucha".

Day 4: Buenos Aires / Iguazu

In Iguazu, nature offers you an unforgettable sight. 900 meters of passages over Rio Iguazu, and the breathtaking Iguazú falls will astonish you.

Day 5: Iguazú / "Garganta del Diablo" or Throat of the Devil

On the "Jungle Train" you begin the ride to the heart of the Iguazu falls. A walk through the passages will allow you to walk among the waterfalls till you reach the "Garganta del Diablo".

Day 6: Brazilian falls

Enjoy the Iguazú falls from the Brazilian side through 900 meters of passages.

Day 7: Iguazú / Puerto Madryn

A very short flight from Iguazú allows you to reach Trelew city, where you begin one of the most surprising adventures: the sightseeing of the Franca Austral Whales in the warm waters of the Golfo Nuevo in front of Puerto Madryn.

Day 8: Puerto Madryn, adventure in the Peninsula

A boat takes you from Puerto Piramides to the blue deep waters of Mar Argentino, where in the month between july and December the whales arrive to procreate and take care of their little ones.

Day 9: Puerto Madryn, penguins

You can observe the big colony of Magallanes penguins in Puerto Madryn, that concentrate in the Patagonic coast from October to March, these are one of the treasures that you can enjoy in the excursion to Punta Tombo.

Day 10: Puerto Madryn / El Calafate

The Glacier air touches your face softly as soon as you get off the plane bringing you from Puerto Madryn. You arrive in El Calafate city, the access to the impressing Perito Moreno Glacier.

Day 11: El Calafate - Perito Moreno Glacier

You reach the glacier walking along the bridges that let you contemplate the magnificent view of the glacier. It is a soundless white river, with blue tones that wait patiently to fall into the "Lago Argentino" Lake.

Day 12: El Calafate - Free day

This region offers lots of options to enjoy The Glaciers National Park.

Day 13: El Calafate / Buenos Aires

The plane brings you back to Buenos Aires. Our package includes a Dinner Tango´s Show at Esquina Carlos Gardel Corner to discover the secrets of the most famous Argentine dance.

Day 14: Buenos Aires, Free day

You can have a different day taking an excursion to Delta, in Tigre, sailing on a special boat through the channels among the island, and having lunch near the river.

Day 15: Buenos Aires, last day

Along your trip you have visited the most amazing regions of this planet. Ezeiza International Airport is your last stop before boarding your international flight. End of our services.
