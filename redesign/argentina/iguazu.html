Iguazú

Argentina · Choose a 3-night stay, or extend to 6 nights with Buenos Aires.

A focused 4-day stay at the Falls.

The program includes the following services:

  • At Iguazú: Lodging in the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers to Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Falls from Argentina and Brazilian's sides.

Day 1: Buenos Aires / Iguazú

In the Airport of Buenos Aires City you will take the flight to Iguazú. You will step red earth and you will start feeling the water drops of the Falls. Transfer to the chosen hotel. According to your flight time you can make any activity or excursion. Yes, you have to wait only half day to visit the water jumps from the Argentinean or the Brazilian side. At night, lodging.

Day 2: Iguazú

Breakfast. In the morning you could visit the water jumps from the Argentinean or the Brazilian side, oo both. They are so impressive that we suggest to do once a day. At night return to the hotel. Lodging.

Day 3: Iguazú

Breakfast. The day is free but the forest and the adventure are waiting for you. Is in The great adventure where you will be nearest from the "Devil's throat" (Garganta del Diablo), or you can go into any of the paths between monkeys and suggestive noises…Before this incredible day you will have a time to taste any typical food and lodging at the hotel.

Day 4: Iguazú / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. With a regional gift you will arrive the Iguazú Airport to flight to the Buenos Aires City. Arrive to the airport. We are pleased to recive you again.

Buenos Aires + Iguazú — 3 nights each, with a city tour and Tango Show.

The program includes the following services:

  • At Buenos Aires: 3 nights of lodging in the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers to Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Tour along the Buenos Aires City.
  • At Iguazú: 3 nights of lodging at the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Falls from Argentina and Brazilian's sides.
  • Dinner Tango's Show.

Day 1: Buenos Aires

Welcome to the Tango's city, we were waiting for you! Once you arrive the International Airport of Ezeiza, you will be taken to the chosen hotel. During the evening it will fulfil a City Tour to take the first contact with the take care. The rest of the day is free to begin feeling the demurrage. At night, lodging.

Day 2: Buenos Aires

Breakfast. It's time to enjoy Buenos Aires night life. Lodging.

Day 3: Buenos Aires / Iguazú

Breakfast. Early in the morning you will take the flight to Iguazu. You will step the typical red soil of Misiones and you almost can be splashed by the Falls. Transfer to the chosen hotel. According to your flight time you can make use of your time to do another activity or excursion. Yes!! With only half of the day you could visit the water jumps on the Argentina's side or on the Brasilian's Side. During night, lodging.

Day 4: Iguazú

Breakfast. During the morning you could visit the water jumps, on the Argentina's side or on the Brasilian's Side, or both of them. As they are so marvelous, we suggest you to visit only one side each day. At night, back to the hotel.

Day 5: Iguazú

Breakfast. Free day but the adventure and the jungle are waiting for you. In The Great Adventure you could be so close as it is possible to the Garganta del Diablo, or maybe you could go into some of the paths among typical flora, monkeys and suggesting sounds. After an incredible day as this, you will have enough time to taste some typical food and lodge at the hotel.

Day 6: Iguazú / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. Surely with a lot of regional gifts, you will we transfer to the Airport of Iguazu to take the flight back to Buenos Aires City. Once you arrive there. In the night Tango Show at Carlos Gardel Corner. Lodging at the chosen hotel.

Day 7: Buenos Aires

Breakfast. According to your flight time, you could still have a walk round Buenos Aires's City. Transfer to the Airport of Buenos Aires.
