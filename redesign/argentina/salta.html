Salta - 4 Days / 3 Nights

Argentina · 3-Night Program

The program includes the following services:

  • At Buenos Aires: Lodging in the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers to Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • City Tour.
  • Excursions to the Humahuaca's gully (Patrimony of the humanity).

Day 1: Buenos Aires / Salta

Arrival to the city of Salta and I transfer to the selected hotel. According to your flight time, you will be able to take advantage to carry out some activity or trip. In the afternoon we suggest you to give, the City Tour. If you don´t prefer that, walk for the streets of the city will be a charm. At night and before going to the hotel, you will be able to have dinner with some typical food and a traditional Folkloric Party. Lodging.

Day 2: Salta

Breakfast. Day off but surely with many activity. Ideal for the accomplishment of the Train to the Clouds by day in the daytime. I complete. In the night I return to the selected hotel and holding.

Day 3: Salta

Breakfast. Day off but surely with many activity. Excursion to Humahuaca's Gully (Patrimony of the Humanity). In the night I return to the selected hotel and housing.

Day 4: Salta / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. According to your flight time, you will be able to take advantage to carry out some excursion or trip. In the morning, or in the afternoon we suggest you to give the Four Track Expedition in San Lorenzo Village, nearest to Salta City. With some gifts of the region, you will move to the Airport of Salta to go to the City of Buenos Aires. Arrival to the Airport of Buenos Aires.
