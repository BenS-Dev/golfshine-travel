Buenos Aires, Puerto Madryn, Ushuaia, El Calafate & Bariloche

Argentina · 18 Days / 16 Nights

The program includes the following services:

  • All destinations: Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Buenos Aires: 4 night accommodation in the chosen hotel with breakfast. A Tour along Buenos Aires City. Dinner Tango´s Show.
  • Puerto Madryn: 3 night accommodation in the chosen hotel with breakfast. Excursión to Punta Tombo with sighseeing of the toninas. Excursion to Peninsula de Valdes with sighseeing of whales.
  • Ushuaia: 3 night accommodation in the chosen hotel with breakfast. Excursion to Lago Fagnano in 4X4 off road car.
  • El Calafate: 3 night accommodation in the chosen hotel with breakfast. Excursion to Perito Moreno Glacier passages.
  • Bariloche: 3 night accommodatio.

Day 1: Europe / America

With the south cross as a guide you fly over the Atlantic Ocean to have an unforgettable experience in Argentina with its incredible scenery and wild nature.

Day 2: Marvellous Buenos Aires

The city tour lets you discover the great city located on the Rio de La Plata. Buenos Aires captures her visitors at first sight, specially its numerous cultural options.

Day 3: Free day in Buenos Aires

Near the city, you can find different lifestyles. On a special country day, you can be indulged with famous "Asado Criollo" and a typical horse shows as a part of the "Fiesta Gaucha".

Day 4: Buenos Aires / Puerto Madryn

A very short flight from Buenos Aires allows you to reach Trelew city, where you begin one of the most surprising adventures: the sightseeing of the Franca Austral Whales in the warm waters of the Golfo Nuevo in front of Puerto Madryn.

Day 5: Puerto Madryn, adventure in the Peninsula

A boat takes you from Puerto Piramides to the blue deep waters of Mar Argentino, where in the month between july and December the whales arrive to procreate and take care of their little ones.

Day 6: Puerto Madryn, penguins

You can observe the big colony of Magallanes penguins in Puerto Madryn, that concentrate in the Patagonic coast from October to March, these are one of the treasures that you can enjoy in the excursion to Punta Tombo.

Day 7: Puerto Madryn / Ushuaia

A very short flight from Trelew allows you to reach the most southern furthest place in the world: Ushuaia, a treat to enjoy throughout the year.

Day 8: Ushuaia, Fagnano Lake off road

The excursion to Fagnano Lake on special 4X4 off road trucks, from Ushuaia city traveling across the Southern Andes, to Garibaldi lake to taste some delicious grilled beef.

Day 9: Ushuaia - Free day

There are several alternatives to enjoy your free day: the splendid excursion to the Tierra del Fuego National Park, or a visit the uninhabited Gable Isle, or - only in winter - a sledge tour pulled by special dogs (huskies).

Day 10: Ushuaia / El Calafate

The Glacier air touches your face softly as soon as you get off the plane bringing you from Ushuaia. You arrive in El Calafate city, the access to the Perito Moreno Glacier.

Day 11: El Calafate - Perito Moreno Glacier

You reach the glacier walking along the bridges that let you contemplate the magnificent view of the glacier, it is a soundless white river, with blue tones that wait patiently to fall into the "Argentino Lake".

Day 12: El Calafate - Free day

This region offers lots of options to enjoy The Glaciers National Park.

Day 13: El Calafate / Bariloche

A very short flight from El Calafate allows you to reach one of the most beautiful mountain villages: San Carlos de Bariloche on the Nahuel Huapi Lake.

Day 14: Bariloche, adventure on the island

Sailing along the picturesque Nahuel Huapi Lake, you reach Isla Victoria and a dreaming forest of arrayan trees. This trip begins at "Puerto Pañuelo", 25 km. from the city, sailing on a very modern boat.

Day 15: Bariloche

On a rented car you can become your own guide, appreciating places that otherwise you would not be able to see. Villa La Angostura captivates you with its amazing little port, and with its Andes architecture.

Day 16: Bariloche / Buenos Aires

The plane brings you back to Buenos Aires. Our package includes a Dinner Tango´s Show at Carlos Gardel Corner, for you to discover the secrets of the most famous Argentine dance.

Day 17: Buenos Aires, Free day

You can have a different day taking an excursion to Delta, in Tigre, sailing on a special boat through the channels among the islands, and have lunch near the river.

Day 18: Buenos Aires, last day

Along your trip you have visited the most marvelous regions of this planet. Ezeiza International Airport is your last stop before boarding your international flight. End of our services.
