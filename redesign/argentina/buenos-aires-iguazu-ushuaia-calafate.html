Buenos Aires, Iguazú, Ushuaia & El Calafate

Argentina · 15 Days / 13 Nights

The program includes the following services:

  • All destinations: Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Buenos Aires: 4 night accommodation in the chosen hotel with breakfast. A Tour along Buenos Aires City. Dinner Tango Show.
  • Iguazu: 3 night accommodation in the chosen hotel with breakfast. Excursion to Iguazu Falls on the Argentinean side and Brazilian side.
  • Ushuaia: 3 night accommodation in the chosen hotel with breakfast. Excursion to Lago Fagnano off road 4X4 with lunch box, and excursion to Beagle Channel.
  • El Calafate: 3 night accommodation in the chosen hotel with breakfast. Excursion to Perito Moreno Glacier passages.

Day 1: Europe / America

With the south cross as a guide you fly over the Atlantic Ocean to have an unforgettable experience in Argentina with its incredible scenery and wild nature. Enjoy your quiet flight over the ocean to take a rest, and to prepare for what comes next.

Day 2: Marvellous Buenos Aires

The city tour lets you discover the great city located on the River of La Plata banks. Buenos Aires captures her visitors at first sight, specially its numerous cultural options. Highlight: "La Reina del Plata", as it was referred to in tango, casts a spell on her guest. Theaters, restaurants, cinemas, bars and coffee shops allow you to discover Buenos Aires's identity.

Day 3: Free day in Buenos Aires

Near the city, you can find different lifestyles. On a special country day, you can be indulged with famous "Asado Criollo" and a typical horse show as a part of a "Fiesta Gaucha". In short, an option you can not miss.

Day 4: Buenos Aires / Iguazu

In Iguazu, nature offers you an unforgetabble sight. 900 meters of passages over Rio Iguazu, and the breathtaking Iguazú falls will astonish you.

Day 5: Iguazú / "Garganta del Diablo" or Throat of the Devil

On the "Jungle Train" you begin the ride to the heart of the Iguazu falls. A walk through the passages will allow you to walk among the waterfalls till you reach the "Garganta del Diablo". The possibility of having a baptism by these spraying waters, on a short trip by boat, is an optional excursion that is very difficult to resist!

Day 6: Brazilian falls

Enjoy the Iguazú falls from the Brazilian side through 900 meters of passages.

Day 7: Iguazú / Ushuaia

A very short flight from Iguazú to Buenos Aires allows you to reach the most southern place in the world: Ushuaia, a treat to enjoy throughout the year.

Day 8: Ushuaia, Fagnano Lake off road

The excursion to Fagnano Lake on special 4X4 off road trucks, from Ushuaia city traveling across the Southern Andes, to Garibaldi lake to taste some delicious grilled meat.

Day 9: Ushuaia — Free day

There are several alternatives to enjoy your free day: the splendid excursion to the Tierra del Fuego National Park, or a visit the uninhabited Gable Isle, or — only in winter — a sledge tour pulled by special dogs (huskies).

Day 10: Ushuaia / El Calafate

The Glacier air touches your face softly as soon as you get off the plane bringing you from Ushuaia. You arrive in "El Calafate" city, the access to the Perito Moreno Glacier.

Day 11: El Calafate — Perito Moreno Glacier

You reach the glacier walking along the bridges that let you contemplate the magnificent view of the glacier, it is a soundless white river, with blue tones that wait patiently to fall into the "Lago Argentino" Lake.

Day 12: El Calafate — Free day

This region offers lots of options to enjoy The Glaciers National Park.

Day 13: El Calafate / Buenos Aires

The plane brings you back to Buenos Aires. Our package includes a Dinner Tango´s Show at Carlos Gardel Corner to discover the secrets of the most famous Argentine dance.

Day 14: Buenos Aires, Free day

You can have a different day taking an excursion to Delta, in Tigre, sailing on a special boat through the channels among the island, and having lunch near the river. Otherwise you can spend the last day doing your last shopping.

Day 15: Buenos Aires, last day

Along your trip you have visited the most amazing.
