El Calafate

Argentina · Choose a 3-night stay, or extend to 6 nights with Buenos Aires.

A focused 4-day stay at the Perito Moreno Glacier.

The program includes the following services:

  • At El Calafate: Lodging at the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Traditional excursion to the Perito Moreno Glacier.

Day 1: Buenos Aires / El Calafate

Breakfast. Transfer to Airport of Buenos Aires, to flight to the Patagonia. You will step El Calafate, almost feeling the ice from the Glaciers. Transfer from Airport to Chosen hotel. The rest of the day is free. According with your flight time, you could take advantage of the time to do any activity or excursion. The El Calafate´s Balcony will give you an introduction just to begin with the adventure. At night and if the weather allows it, you won't forget what a wonderful sky full of starts. Lodging.

Day 2: El Calafate

Yes, first of all, your breakfast; because the Perito Moreno's Glacier is not going to escape from us! Very good sheltered and with photo camera prepared for taking a lot of pictures, you'll visit it. Impressive, incredible, isn't it? It wouldn't be easy but you'll have to return. Consult us because in the Estancia Alice you could taste a typical Patagonia barbecue, or even have the continental ices so close to you, with a Walk right upon the Perito Moreno Glacier. Don't worry, you still have another day there. Lodging at the hotel.

Day 3: El Calafate

Breakfast. Your day is completely free, but that would be an error. You have a lot of things to do and our suggestions will froze you: in Upsala Explorer you'll be circled by floes, glaciers, patagonic winds and adventure without limit. It will be a day that you'll never forget during the rest of your life. At night, lodging at the hotel.

Day 4: El Calafate / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. According with your flight time, you could take advantage of your time to have a walk. Transfer from the hotel to Ezeiza International Airport.
