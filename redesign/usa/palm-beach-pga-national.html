PGA National Resort & Spa — Palm Beach

USA · Florida · Palm Beach · 3 Nights · 3 Rounds

Enjoy a luxurious stay at the PGA National Resort & Spa in Palm Beach. Immerse yourself in spectacular natural beauty, exquisite cuisine, pristine beaches, inviting pools and exciting championship golf. Our exclusive package gives you the opportunity to play renowned PGA National golf courses such as Fazio, Estate, Palmer or Champion, each with its own distinction and unique challenges. Experience the prowess of these exceptionally designed courses and feel the breeze of the Atlantic Ocean just steps away. Enjoy the wonderful Palm Beach area with phenomenal golf and all the resort amenities you could want.

This Stay & Play Package includes

  • 3 Nights Accommodation Resort View Room with Breakfast.
  • 3 Rounds with cart on: The Fazio, The Estate, The Palmer, The Champion.
  • Complimentary replay round greens fees on Palmer.
  • Full breakfast daily.
  • Also includes Taxes & Daily Resort Fees.
