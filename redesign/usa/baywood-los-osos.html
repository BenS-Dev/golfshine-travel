Baywood Los Osos

USA · California · Central Coast

Welcome to Baywood Los Osos, a magical destination for golf enthusiasts located in the picturesque Central Coast of California. This charming coastal community fulfills the dreams of those seeking an unparalleled golfing experience, surrounded by a stunning natural environment. The golf course will amaze you with its lush fairways and well-kept greens, while the gentle Pacific Ocean breezes accompany you throughout your game.

The course design offers exciting challenges, from strategic bunkers to serene lakes, putting your skills and strategy to the test at every hole. In addition to golf, Baywood Los Osos offers warm local hospitality and a delightful culinary scene to enjoy after your rounds.

If you're looking for an unforgettable golfing experience in a unique setting, come and discover the magic of Baywood Los Osos on California's Central Coast. We await you to create memorable moments in this coastal paradise!
