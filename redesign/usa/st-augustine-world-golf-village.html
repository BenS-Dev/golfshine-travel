World Golf Village — St Augustine

USA · Florida · 3 Nights · 3 Rounds

Stay and Play Package for World Golf Village in St. Augustine, Florida — one of the top golf destinations in the state! This is a fantastic opportunity to play 3 rounds, on great courses designed by legends of the game — Palmer & Nicklaus, Sarazen & Snead!

Our World Golf Village Package includes

  • 3 Nights at Holiday Inn St. Augustine — World Golf Village.
  • 3 Rounds with cart at: King and Bear Course — Play the only single course in the world co-designed by Hall of Famer's Arnold Palmer and Jack Nicklaus.
  • Slammer and Squire — Designed by: Bobby Weed, Sam Snead and Gene Sarazen.
  • St. Johns Golf & Country Club — Clyde Johnston gem; host course for numerous PGA Tour Q-School qualifiers and Florida PGA events.

Notes: Includes Daily resort fee and applicable package fees. Extra nights available. Other courses may be added.
