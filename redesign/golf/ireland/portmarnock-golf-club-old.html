Portmarnock Golf Club (Old)

East Ireland · Ireland

The Portmarnock Hotel sits on Ireland's stunning east coast, in the heart of Ireland's golfing region. The hotel boasts an internationally acclaimed 18-hole golf course which is considered to be one of Ireland's premier courses. It offers 98 luxurious guest rooms, all elegantly decorated and designed to ensure guest benefit from either sea views or views across the golf course. All rooms offer the facilities needed to ensure guests experience the utmost in luxury. A fitness room and health spa are also available.
