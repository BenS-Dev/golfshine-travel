Tralee Golf Club

South West Ireland · Ireland

Par 71, yardage 6,799. The great Arnold Palmer was involved in designing Tralee Golf Club and, boy, is it a gem! Palmer had always wanted to design a course on the Emerald Isle and that chance came in the early 1980s, the American star working with Ed Seay to create this links masterpiece. There are some great holes on the course, notably the second, which runs along the clifftops, and the third, which has been likened to the famous seventh hole at Pebble Beach. Visitors can play at Tralee without restrictions during the week. On Saturday, they can only start between 11 AM and 1:30 PM while Sunday is out altogether.

Adare Manor Hotel & Golf Resort is one of Ireland's leading 5-star castle properties tucked into the natural seclusion of its 840 acre estate and set beside the picturesque village of Adare in Co. Limerick. It is only a 40 minute drive from Shannon International Airport. Manor House is a highly impressive hotel and the course is one of Ireland's best secrets. The hotel offers world class facilities which include a variety of accommodations to suit your needs: 63 elegantly decorated bedrooms, two first class restaurants, 3 entertaining bars. Other facilities include an indoor heated swimming pool, spa, fitness room etc.

Suggested hotel: Adare Manor Hotel & Golf Resort
