Das Achental Golf

Grassau · Germany

Embark on a journey to discover the extraordinary allure of Das Achental Golf, a destination that seamlessly combines idyllic Alpine charm with an exceptional golfing experience, complemented by a stay at the luxurious Das Achental accommodation.

Nestled within the breathtaking embrace of the Bavarian Alps, Das Achental presents a haven where the splendor of nature converges with world-class golf. A sanctuary for avid golfers and nature enthusiasts alike, this destination promises an unparalleled retreat.

The accommodations at Das Achental offer a harmonious fusion of contemporary elegance and the rustic beauty of the surroundings. Each meticulously designed room provides a serene escape, inviting you to unwind after a day of golfing or exploration amidst the stunning landscapes.

At the heart of this destination lies the meticulously crafted 18-hole golf course, an epitome of design that challenges and inspires golfers of all levels. Set against the backdrop of majestic mountains, the course offers an unforgettable journey through undulating terrains and scenic vistas.

For moments of sheer indulgence, the spa at Das Achental welcomes you with open arms. Delight in an array of revitalizing treatments while taking in panoramic views that rejuvenate the spirit. And when culinary desires beckon, the resort's restaurant offers a delectable selection of local and international cuisine, catering to every palate.

Das Achental beckons you to immerse yourself in a golfing haven that seamlessly blends sophistication with the untouched beauty of the Bavarian Alps. Whether you seek an unparalleled golfing adventure or a tranquil escape, our resort promises an enriching experience that captures the essence of this remarkable region. Surrender to the allure of Das Achental and créate cherished memories that will linger long after your stay.
