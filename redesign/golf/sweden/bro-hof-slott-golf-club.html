Bro Hof Slott Golf Club

Stockholm · Sweden

Welcome to the ultimate golf and luxury retreat at Bro Hof Slott Golf Club, where the art of golf meets the art of hospitality. Nestled in the serene landscapes just outside Stockholm, Sweden, this prestigious golf club offers an unrivaled experience for both golf enthusiasts and those seeking a refined getaway.

Bro Hof Slott Golf Club is more than just a golfing destination; it's an immersive journey into elegance and leisure. The club boasts two championship courses meticulously designed to challenge and inspire golfers of all skill levels. Breathtaking fairways, pristine greens, and stunning water features create an enchanting backdrop for an exceptional game.

But the experience doesn't stop at the last hole. The on-site accommodation, within the elegant castle itself, elevates your stay to a realm of opulence. Indulge in regal comfort with tastefully adorned rooms and suites, each exuding a blend of historic charm and modern luxury.

Beyond golf and accommodation, the club presents an array of exquisite dining options, wellness facilities, and leisure activities to complete your stay. Immerse yourself in the spa's tranquility, savor gourmet cuisine, and explore the beauty of the surrounding nature.

Whether you're a passionate golfer aiming to conquer world-class courses or a discerning traveler seeking refined indulgence, Bro Hof Slott Golf Club delivers. It's a destination where the love for golf meets the allure of a grand castle retreat. Experience the seamless fusion of sport, luxury, and relaxation, where every swing and every moment are imbued with a touch of splendor.
