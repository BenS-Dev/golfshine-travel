Rio Real Golf & Hotel

Marbella · Spain

Welcome to the ultimate golfing paradise: Rio Real Golf & Hotel in Marbella! Embark on an unforgettable journey where lush fairways and breathtaking landscapes meet luxurious accommodations, creating a haven for golf enthusiasts and leisure seekers alike.

Nestled in the heart of Marbella, Rio Real Golf offers a championship 18-hole golf course designed by the legendary Javier Arana. Immerse yourself in a golfer's dream, where each swing is a step towards mastering the art of precision and skill. With its undulating terrain, strategic bunkers, and stunning sea views, this course promises an exhilarating challenge for players of all levels.

But the experience doesn't end on the greens. Our exclusive on-site accommodations at Rio Real Hotel provide the perfect blend of comfort and elegance. Each room is a sanctuary of relaxation, featuring modern amenities, stylish décor, and panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea. After a day on the course, unwind by the poolside with a refreshing cocktail or rejuvenate your senses at our spa and wellness center.

Indulge your palate with exquisite culinary delights at our restaurant, where local flavors and international cuisine blend seamlessly to create a memorable dining experience.

Beyond the fairways, Marbella beckons you to explore its charming Old Town, pristine beaches, and vibrant nightlife. Whether you're a dedicated golfer or seeking a luxurious getaway, Rio Real Golf & Hotel promises an idyllic escape where the art of golf and the allure of Marbella combine to create a truly remarkable adventure.

Book your stay today and discover a world where golfing excellence meets unparalleled luxury. Your journey to Rio Real starts here.
