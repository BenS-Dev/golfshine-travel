Los Monteros Golf

Marbella · Spain

Introducing Los Monteros Spa & Golf Resort - an enchanting retreat nestled amid Marbella's golfing paradise. Discover a world where golf and luxury converge seamlessly, inviting you to embark on a captivating journey through lush fairways and refined elegance.

Indulge in the art of relaxation as you step into a realm of refined opulence. Our resort boasts a collection of 79 captivating rooms and suites, each meticulously designed to marry modern sophistication with a touch of artistic allure. Unwind in the embrace of our oversized beds, rejuvenate under invigorating power showers, and immerse yourself in the ambiance of our creatively adorned spaces that pay homage to Marbella's rich heritage.

For golf aficionados seeking a sanctuary, Los Monteros Spa & Golf Resort beckons. Tee off against the backdrop of Marbella's stunning landscapes, knowing that a warm and inviting welcome awaits your return. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the course - it envelops you in an atmosphere of comfort and charm.

Allow Los Monteros Spa & Golf Resort to be your haven, your base for exploration, and your partner in creating unforgettable memories amidst Marbella's captivating fairways. Your golfing adventure will be elevated not only by the challenging courses you conquer but also by the unparalleled luxury and style that define our walls.

Experience the ultimate fusion of golf and luxury at Los Monteros Spa & Golf Resort, where each swing and every moment become an ode to indulgence and exquisite living. Your journey through Marbella's golfing wonders starts here, where elegance and fairways unite for an exceptional escape.
