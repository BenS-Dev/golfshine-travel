Corfu Golf Club

Corfu · Greece

Welcome to Corfu Golf Club | International Golf Course, a golfer's haven nestled in the captivating landscapes of the Greek island of Corfu. Beyond a mere golf course, it's a sanctuary where lush nature harmonizes with meticulously designed fairways, offering an extraordinary escape for golf enthusiasts and novices alike.

Corfu Golf Club boasts an exceptional 18-hole course, set against a backdrop of rolling hills and breathtaking sea views. With each swing, you'll embrace the stunning scenery. As you tee off amidst verdant greens and azure skies, each stroke becomes a testament to your passion and skill.

Whether refining your technique or embarking on a golfing journey, Corfu Golf Club offers a welcoming ambiance complemented by expert guidance. The practice facilities and scenic vistas create an immersive golfing experience that lingers in memory.

Beyond the greens, discover refined comfort at Atlantica Grand Mediterraneo Resort and Spa. This elegant haven complements your golfing adventure with opulent relaxation, perfectly nestled in the enchanting surroundings of Corfu.

Step into exquisitely designed rooms and suites, each an oasis of luxury and tranquility. Greet the day with stunning sea views or serene garden landscapes, offering a serene escape.

Savor culinary excellence at the resort's restaurants, where international flavors and local cuisine unite to create a symphony for your taste buds. From gourmet feasts to casual delights, every dish is a testament to culinary artistry.

Indulge in the rejuvenating world of the spa, where ancient rituals blend with modern therapies to create a holistic wellness experience.

Discover the beauty of Corfu, known for its rich history and captivating landscapes. The resort's proximity to local attractions ensures your stay is infused with both cultural exploration and leisure.

Whether perfecting your swing at Corfu Golf Club or immersing yourself in the luxury of Atlantica Grand Mediterraneo Resort and Spa, this seamless fusion of golf and opulence promises an unforgettable escape. Your journey unfolds here, where every stroke, every moment, and every experience are enriched by the captivating landscapes and warm hospitality of Corfu.

Suggested hotel: Atlantica Grand Mediterraneo Resort
