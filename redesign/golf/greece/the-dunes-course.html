The Dunes Course

Costa Navarino · Greece

Welcome to The Dunes Course at Costa Navarino, a golfer's utopia nestled along the captivating coastline of Greece. More than a golf course, it's a sanctuary where natural beauty converges with meticulously designed fairways, offering an enchanting escape for golf enthusiasts and novices alike.

Designed by the legendary Bernhard Langer in collaboration with European Golf Design, The Dunes Course presents an exceptional 18-hole journey. With each swing, you harmonize with the stunning surroundings. As you tee off amidst undulating dunes and panoramic sea views, every stroke becomes a testament to your passion and skill.

Whether honing your technique or embarking on your golfing journey, The Dunes Course delivers a welcoming atmosphere complemented by expert guidance. The practice facilities and breathtaking vistas offer an immersive golfing experience that lingers in memory.

Beyond the greens, discover opulence at The Romanos, a Luxury Collection Resort in Costa Navarino. Nestled in lush surroundings, this haven epitomizes luxury. Lavishly appointed rooms and suites exude elegance, inviting you to unwind and embrace comfort.

The resort's culinary offerings are a celebration of gastronomic delights. From delectable local dishes to international cuisine, each meal is a symphony of flavors designed to tantalize your senses.

Unwind at the world-class Anazoe Spa, where rejuvenation is elevated to an art form. Indulge in bespoke treatments inspired by ancient Greek rituals and modern wellness practices.

The Romanos Resort's commitment to excellence extends to its surroundings, allowing you to explore Costa Navarino's natural beauty and cultural treasures.

Whether perfecting your swing at The Dunes Course or indulging in the luxurious embrace of The Romanos Resort, this harmonious fusion of golf and opulence promises an unforgettable escape. Your journey unfolds here, where every stroke, every moment, and every experience are enhanced by the captivating landscapes and gracious hospitality of Costa Navarino.

Suggested hotel: The Westin Resort
