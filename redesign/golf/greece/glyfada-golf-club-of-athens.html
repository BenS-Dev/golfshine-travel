Glyfada Golf Club of Athens

Athens · Greece

Welcome to Porto Carras Golf & Country Club, a golfer's paradise nestled along the stunning shores of Greece. More than just a golf course, it's a sanctuary where natural beauty intertwines with meticulously designed fairways, offering an exceptional escape for golf enthusiasts and novices alike.

Porto Carras Golf & Country Club presents a world-class 18-hole course, set against a backdrop of rolling hills and mesmerizing sea views. With every swing, you'll embrace the stunning scenery. As you tee off amidst lush landscapes and azure skies, each stroke becomes a testament to your passion and skill.

Whether refining your technique or embarking on a golfing journey, Porto Carras Golf & Country Club offers a welcoming ambiance paired with expert guidance. The practice facilities and scenic vistas create an immersive golfing experience that lingers in memory.

Beyond the greens, embrace lavish comfort at Porto Carras Grand Resort, seamlessly blending elegance with breathtaking nature. This opulent haven perfectly complements your golfing adventure, offering a world of relaxation and indulgence.

Step into exquisitely appointed rooms and suites, each designed to cocoon you in luxury and style. Whether greeted by stunning sea views or serene garden landscapes, every space offers a sanctuary to unwind.

Indulge in culinary delights at the resort's restaurants, where flavors from around the world unite to create a symphony for your palate. From gourmet creations to casual favorites, each dish is a testament to culinary artistry.

Explore the enchanting surroundings of Sithonia, from pristine beaches to cultural treasures. The resort's proximity to local attractions ensures your stay is immersed in both exploration and leisure.

Whether perfecting your swing at Porto Carras Golf & Country Club or indulging in the luxury of Porto Carras Grand Resort, this seamless fusion of golf and opulence promises an unforgettable escape. Your journey unfolds here, where every stroke, every moment, and every experience are enhanced by the captivating landscapes and warm hospitality of Greece.

Suggested hotel: Glyfada Riviera Hotel
