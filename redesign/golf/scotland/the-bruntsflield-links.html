The Bruntsflield Links

Edinburgh · Scotland

Founded in 1761, the Bruntsfield Links Golfing Society stands as the fourth oldest golf club globally and ranks among Scotland's most esteemed clubs. Situated merely 3 miles northwest of Edinburgh city centre and a convenient 1-hour distance from The Old Course in St. Andrews, it boasts a mature parkland-style course. Originally crafted by Willie Park Jnr, the course underwent subsequent modifications by the eminent architects Dr. Alister MacKenzie and James Braid. The testament to its course quality lies in its designation as a Qualifying Venue for the Open Championship by The R&A since 2011.

Enter the epitome of all Malmaisons. Once a house of questionable repute, it has now earned an unparalleled repute itself. A short taxi jaunt from Waverley Station places Malmaison Edinburgh at Leith (as they say up here). It’s a stone's throw from the Royal Yacht Britannia, but what it offers is a generous dose of contemporary luxury. Yes, when the quest is for captivating lodgings in Edinburgh, this reigns supreme, even over the castle. Our Edinburgh hotel situates you proximate to the marvels of Edinburgh's cobbled streets, astonishing shopping opportunities, and renowned art galleries. Then it's time to return to Malmaison for a Scottish dining experience with a twist, an array of single malt whiskies and mojitos await at the bar, followed by retreat to the most stylish and splendid rooms in Scotland's capital.

From its 18th-century inception, the Bruntsfield Links Golfing Society has upheld a distinguished tradition in the world of golf. The meticulously kept and challenging grounds stand as a tribute to the craftsmanship of several acclaimed designers. Seamlessly blending Scotland's natural landscapes with Edinburgh's accessibility, it beckons both golf enthusiasts and luxury seekers. Malmaison Edinburgh, on the other hand, has artfully transformed its historical past into a matchless hotel encounter, presenting guests with an ambiance of contemporary elegance within a milieu steeped in history and culture.

Suggested hotel: Malmoison Edinburgh
