Oporto Golf Club

Oporto · Portugal

Experience golfing excellence and coastal luxury at Oporto Golf Club in the picturesque city of Oporto, Portugal. Immerse yourself in a world of golfing perfection and embrace the allure of the Atlantic coastline. Nestled in the heart of Oporto, this prestigious golf club offers an exceptional blend of challenging fairways and stunning sea views.

As you tee off on meticulously manicured greens, the gentle ocean breeze carries the promise of an unforgettable round. The Oporto Golf Club is renowned for its classic yet intricate course design, catering to golfers of all skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a beginner, each swing is an opportunity to relish the game amidst breathtaking surroundings.

Indulge in the ultimate golf retreat with a stay at Exe Praia Golfe, a luxurious 5-star haven just moments away. This exquisite beachfront hotel welcomes you to unwind in lavish comfort after a day on the fairways. Impeccably appointed rooms and suites offer a seamless blend of modern elegance and coastal tranquility, ensuring a rejuvenating escape.

Discover the city's rich history and vibrant culture, explore charming streets, and savor delectable Portuguese cuisine. Oporto Golf Club's strategic location allows you to seamlessly transition from an invigorating game to a captivating city adventure.

Elevate your golfing journey and create lasting memories at Oporto Golf Club, where the allure of the fairways meets the enchantment of the ocean. With the added allure of an opulent stay at Exe Praia Golfe, this is more than a golfing experience; it's a gateway to coastal indulgence and cultural exploration.

Suggested hotel: Exe Praia Golfe
