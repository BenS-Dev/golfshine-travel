Palmares Ocean Living & Golf

Lagos · Portugal

Prepare to embark on an extraordinary golfing journey at Palmares Ocean Living & Golf. This spectacular 27-hole championship course, designed by Robert Trent Jones Jr., promises an unforgettable experience that seamlessly marries challenge and natural beauty. As you navigate through rolling fairways, towering sand dunes, and breathtaking sea views, each swing becomes an artful expression of the game.

Indulge in coastal luxury at the Palmares Beach House Hotel, a haven of sophistication overlooking the ocean. Each room and suite is a sanctuary of modern elegance, offering a seamless blend of comfort and style. From your private balcony, savor panoramic views of the Atlantic, and unwind amidst a setting that captures the essence of beachside relaxation. Beyond the fairways, Palmares invites you to explore the charm of the Algarve region. Discover the historic town of Lagos, indulge in the flavors of Portuguese cuisine, or simply bask in the sun on pristine beaches. Engage in water sports, explore local markets, or revel in the vibrant nightlife that defines this coastal haven.

Unleash your passion for golf against the backdrop of Portugal's breathtaking coastline at Palmares Ocean Living & Golf. Secure your golf and accommodation package today, and embark on a journey where luxury and sporting excellence converge seamlessly. Your dream golf escape awaits at Palmares and the Beach House Hotel.

Suggested hotel: Palmares Beach House Hotel
