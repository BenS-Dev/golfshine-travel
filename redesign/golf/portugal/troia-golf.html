Troia Golf

Troia · Portugal

Experience a golfing haven like no other at Troia Golf, nestled along the breathtaking shores of Portugal. Prepare to embark on an extraordinary golfing journey, paired with luxurious accommodations that redefine coastal living.

Immerse yourself in the world of golfing excellence at Troia Golf, a true gem of the region. Designed to harmonize with the stunning natural landscapes, this championship course offers an unforgettable blend of challenge and beauty. As you swing through meticulously crafted fairways and navigate through sand dunes, you'll be treated to mesmerizing views of the Atlantic Ocean and the surrounding Arrábida Natural Park.

Elevate your stay with a selection of exceptional accommodations within close proximity to Troia Golf. Choose from a range of modern apartments and elegant villas, each designed to provide the ultimate retreat after a day on the greens. Unwind on private terraces, bask in luxurious interiors, and revel in the tranquility that defines this coastal escape.

Beyond the greens, Troia invites you to explore the charms of the region. Delight in the local flavors of Portuguese cuisine, explore historic towns, or simply relax on pristine beaches kissed by the sun. Engage in outdoor adventures, pamper yourself at spas, and immerse yourself in the vibrant culture that makes Portugal a sought-after destination.

Suggested hotel: Troia Resort
