Royal Park Roveri

Turin · Italy

Embark on an unparalleled golfing journey at Royal Park Roveri, a jewel nestled within the captivating landscapes of Piemonte, Italy. Here, golf transcends sport, evolving into a symphony of skill and natural beauty that unfolds across fairways embraced by rolling hills.

The meticulously designed 36-hole course at Royal Park Roveri offers a harmonious blend of challenge and grandeur. As you navigate the course, surrounded by lush greenery and panoramic vistas of the Piedmontese countryside, each swing becomes a masterful brushstroke on a canvas of artful play.

Golfers of all calibers find their haven at Royal Park Roveri, where seasoned instructors and world-class facilities ensure an enriching experience. Every tee-off is an opportunity to elevate your technique and immerse yourself in the essence of golf.

Beyond the greens, discover a realm of regal tranquility at Principi di Piemonte, a lavish retreat exuding timeless elegance in the heart of Turin. This opulent sanctuary seamlessly marries historic charm with modern luxury, offering a haven of indulgence.

Enter luxuriously appointed rooms and suites, each a testament to sophistication and comfort. Gaze out upon enchanting views of Turin's architectural wonders or the serene garden, setting the stage for an unforgettable respite.

Rejuvenate your senses at the hotel's spa, where lavish treatments and wellness rituals whisk you away to a world of serenity. Delight your palate at the restaurant, where culinary artistry meets exceptional flavors against a backdrop of regal opulence.

As you explore the cultural treasures of Turin, from majestic palaces to vibrant piazzas, you'll discover that your stay at Principi di Piemonte is not merely an escape; it's an immersion into the heart of Italian splendor and hospitality. Here, the allure of golf and the luxury of accommodation unite seamlessly, crafting an experience that resonates in your memory. Your Piemontese getaway begins here.

Suggested hotel: Principi di Piemonte
