Stavanger Golfklubb

Stavanger · Norway

Discover a golfer's paradise at Stavanger Golfklubb, nestled amidst the captivating landscapes of Norway. Here, golf enthusiasts are welcomed to a world of pristine fairways, breathtaking views, and a sense of serenity that revitalizes the spirit.

Stavanger Golfklubb boasts an exceptional 18-hole course, thoughtfully designed to harmonize with the surrounding Norwegian beauty. As you tee off against the backdrop of rolling hills and serene lakes, each swing becomes a testament to the fusion of skill and nature.

After an invigorating day on the greens, bask in the urban comfort of Stavanger Forum Hotel, conveniently located near the golf course. The hotel offers a stylish escape where modern design meets Norwegian hospitality, providing a perfect balance between relaxation and sophistication.

Recharge in elegantly appointed rooms and suites, each providing a haven of comfort and tranquility. Whether gazing at city views or the serene coastline, you'll find yourself immersed in a realm of urban respite.

Indulge your senses at the hotel's spa, where rejuvenating treatments and wellness rituals offer a sanctuary of serenity. Delight your palate at the hotel's restaurant, where culinary delights await to tantalize your taste buds.

Whether perfecting your swing on the greens or unwinding in the modern embrace of Stavanger Forum Hotel, this harmonious blend of golf and urban luxury promises an unforgettable escape. Your journey unfolds at Stavanger Golfklubb and Stavanger Forum Hotel, where every moment reflects the allure of Norway's landscapes and the comfort of urban hospitality.

Suggested hotel: Stavanger Forum Hotel
