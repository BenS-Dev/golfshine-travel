Vestfold Golf Club

Tønsberg · Norway

Step into a golfer's paradise at Vestfold Golf Club, nestled amidst the captivating landscapes of Norway. More than just a golf course, it's a sanctuary of natural beauty, meticulously designed fairways, and a serene escape that beckons both seasoned golfers and beginners.

Vestfold Golf Club proudly presents its exceptional 18-hole course, where every swing is a dance with nature. As you tee off against a backdrop of rolling hills and tranquil lakes, the serenity of the surroundings enriches every moment, turning each stroke into a testament of your passion.

Whether refining your skills or embracing the joy of golf for the first time, Vestfold Golf Club offers a welcoming atmosphere with expert guidance. The practice facilities and scenic vistas create an immersive experience, leaving an indelible mark.

Beyond the greens, experience luxurious comfort at Oslofjord Hotel, conveniently located near Vestfold Golf Club. This modern retreat seamlessly blends elegance with Norwegian charm, offering a haven that perfectly complements your golfing adventure.

Step into beautifully appointed rooms and suites, each designed for relaxation and style. Whether you're greeted by fjord views or serene gardens, every space provides a sanctuary to unwind.

Indulge in exceptional dining at the hotel's restaurant, where local flavors and international cuisine unite in a symphony for your palate. From gourmet creations to casual delights, each dish is a tribute to culinary artistry.

Explore the cultural wonders of Vestfold and the Oslofjord region, known for its rich history and picturesque landscapes. The hotel's proximity to local attractions ensures that your stay is immersed in both exploration and leisure.

Whether perfecting your swing at Vestfold Golf Club or savoring the comfort of Oslofjord Hotel, this harmonious blend of golf and relaxation promises an unforgettable escape. Your journey unfolds here, where every stroke and every moment are enriched by the captivating landscapes and warm hospitality of Norway.

Suggested hotel: Oslofjord Hotel
