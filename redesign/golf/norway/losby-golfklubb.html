Losby Golfklubb

Oslo · Norway

Discover a golfing haven like no other at Losby Golfpark, nestled in the breathtaking landscapes of Norway. Here, golf enthusiasts and relaxation seekers alike find themselves immersed in a world of natural beauty, impeccable fairways, and a sense of serenity that rejuvenates the soul.

Losby Golfpark offers an exceptional 27-hole golf course, meticulously designed to blend seamlessly with the stunning Norwegian surroundings. As you tee off amidst lush greens and scenic vistas, each swing becomes a moment of sheer bliss, accompanied by the gentle rustling of leaves and the soothing whispers of nature.

After a day on the greens, indulge in the opulent embrace of Losby Gods, an enchanting hotel harmoniously nestled within the golfing paradise. Steeped in historic charm and modern elegance, Losby Gods offers a sanctuary of tranquility where every detail is designed to elevate your stay.

Unwind in sumptuously appointed rooms and suites, each adorned with timeless décor and offering views that capture the essence of Norwegian beauty. Immerse yourself in leisure at the hotel's spa, where rejuvenating treatments and wellness rituals transport you to a realm of relaxation.

Delight your palate with exquisite culinary experiences at Losby Gods' restaurant, where locally sourced ingredients are transformed into gastronomic delights that tantalize the senses.

Whether you're perfecting your swing on the greens or savoring moments of pure indulgence at Losby Gods, this harmonious union of golf and luxury promises an unforgettable escape. Your journey awaits at Losby Golfpark and Losby Gods, where every stroke and every moment are infused with the magic of Norway's captivating landscapes and gracious hospitality.

Suggested hotel: Losby Gods
