Drøbak Golfklubb

Drøbak · Norway

Step into a golfer's paradise at Drøbak Golfklubb, nestled within Norway's idyllic landscapes. Beyond a mere golf course, it's a sanctuary of natural beauty, meticulously crafted fairways, and a tranquil escape that invites both novices and enthusiasts.

Drøbak Golfklubb proudly presents its exceptional 18-hole course, where every swing is a dance with nature. As you tee off amidst rolling hills and serene lakes, the tranquil backdrop enhances each moment, turning every stroke into a testament of your passion.

Whether refining your skills or embracing golf's joy for the first time, Drøbak Golfklubb offers a welcoming atmosphere with expert guidance. The practice facilities and scenic vistas offer an immersive experience, leaving an enduring impact.

Transition from the greens to refined luxury at Reenskaug Hotel, a graceful retreat near Drøbak Golfklubb. Combining classic charm with modern amenities, the historic hotel creates an oasis amidst Norway's allure.

Step into elegantly appointed rooms and suites, each a haven of comfort and style. City views or serene garden scenes greet you, providing spaces to unwind.

Delight in culinary excellence at the hotel's restaurant, where local and international flavors unite in a symphony for your palate. From gourmet delights to casual bites, every dish is a tribute to culinary artistry.

Explore Drøbak's charming maritime heritage and coastal allure. The hotel's proximity to local attractions ensures a stay infused with culture and leisure.

Whether perfecting your swing at Drøbak Golfklubb or embracing luxury at Reenskaug Hotel, this harmonious fusion of golf and comfort promises an unforgettable escape. Your journey unfolds here, where each stroke and every moment are enriched by the enchanting landscapes and gracious hospitality of Norway.

Suggested hotel: Reenskaug Hotel
