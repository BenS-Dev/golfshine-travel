Himmerland Golf & Spa Resort

Himmerland · Denmark

Welcome to the pinnacle of golf and relaxation at Himmerland Golf & Spa Resort, where the art of golf seamlessly blends with the luxury of on-site accommodation. Nestled in the serene landscapes of northern Denmark, this extraordinary resort offers a golfing experience that transcends the ordinary.

Himmerland Golf & Spa Resort is a haven for golf enthusiasts seeking the perfect fusion of challenging fairways and refined comfort. With two championship courses that have hosted prestigious European Tour events, you'll find yourself in the company of legends as you navigate meticulously designed greens against a backdrop of breathtaking beauty.

But the experience goes beyond the greens. The resort's exquisite accommodation, embodied by the Himmerland Golf & Spa Hotel, promises a seamless transition from the fairways to the lap of luxury. Elegantly appointed rooms and suites provide the perfect sanctuary to rejuvenate after a day on the course.

Indulgence extends beyond golf, as the resort offers a state-of-the-art spa, culinary delights at on-site restaurants, and a range of recreational activities. Whether you're refining your swing on the golf course or unwinding at the spa, every moment is designed to elevate your senses and soothe your soul.

Himmerland Golf & Spa Resort isn't just a destination; it's an experience that caters to golfers and travelers alike. Whether you're a passionate golfer aiming to conquer world-class courses or seeking a refined escape with all-encompassing luxury, Himmerland offers an unparalleled opportunity. Immerse yourself in the seamless blend of sport, relaxation, and sophistication that defines this remarkable resort, and let every stroke of your game be matched by the elegance of your stay.
