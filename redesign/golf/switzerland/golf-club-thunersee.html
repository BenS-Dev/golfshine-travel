Golf Club Thunersee

Thun · Switzerland

Discover a perfect blend of golfing excellence and serene luxury with our exclusive package, showcasing the esteemed Golf Club Thunersee paired with a tranquil stay at the exquisite Hotel Eden Spiez.

Nestled against the backdrop of the mesmerizing Swiss Alps, the Golf Club Thunersee offers a golfing experience that transcends the ordinary. Immerse yourself in the challenge of their meticulously designed fairways, navigate through undulating greens, and take in the stunning panoramic views of Lake Thun that accompany your game.

After an exhilarating day on the course, retreat to the enchanting Hotel Eden Spiez, where modern comfort harmonizes seamlessly with the surrounding natural beauty. Each elegantly appointed room provides a sanctuary of relaxation, ensuring you wake up rejuvenated for another day of golf.

Delight your senses at the hotel's dining venues, where gourmet creations tantalize your taste buds. Unwind at the hotel's wellness area, boasting rejuvenating treatments and a tranquil atmosphere that captures the essence of the Swiss Alps.

The charming town of Spiez offers a delightful array of experiences, from lakeside strolls to cultural discoveries. The convenient location of the Hotel Eden Spiez allows you to explore the town's offerings effortlessly.

Our Golf Club Thunersee package invites you to embrace the synergy of golfing excellence and luxurious accommodation. Whether you're a devoted golfer seeking the perfect swing or a discerning traveler craving relaxation, this extraordinary offer promises an unforgettable experience. Elevate your Swiss getaway with championship-level golf and refined living, curating memories that will linger long after you depart.

Suggested hotel: Hotel Eden Spiez
