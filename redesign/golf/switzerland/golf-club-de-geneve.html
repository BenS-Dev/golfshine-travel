Golf Club de Geneve

Genève · Switzerland

Embark on an exceptional journey that seamlessly combines the allure of golfing mastery with the comfort of a luxurious stay at the prestigious Golf Club de Genève, accompanied by a relaxing retreat at the nearby Hôtel Century.

Nestled in the heart of the captivating Swiss city, Golf Club de Genève stands as a testament to excellence in golf. Immerse yourself in the artistry of the game as you navigate the meticulously designed fairways and greens, each strategically crafted to challenge and inspire golfers of all levels. With stunning vistas of the surrounding landscapes, every swing becomes a memorable experience.

After a day of exhilarating golf, indulge in the tranquility of Hôtel Century. Located in close proximity to the golf course, this elegant haven offers a seamless blend of contemporary comfort and sophistication. Each well-appointed room provides a sanctuary of relaxation, ensuring you wake up refreshed and ready to take on another round.

Dine in style at the hotel's exquisite restaurant, where culinary delights await to satisfy your palate. Unwind at the hotel's wellness facilities, a haven of rejuvenation that captures the essence of relaxation.

Explore the cultural and scenic wonders of Geneva, a city known for its elegance and vibrancy. The hotel's strategic location allows you to effortlessly discover the city's treasures.

Our Golf Club de Genève package invites you to immerse yourself in a world where golfing prowess meets opulent accommodation. Whether you're a passionate golfer seeking the ultimate swing or a discerning traveler in pursuit of refined luxury, this exceptional offering promises an unforgettable experience. Elevate your Swiss escape with the excellence of golf and the comfort of Hôtel Century, crafting cherished memories that will linger long after your departure.

Suggested hotel: Hôtel Century
