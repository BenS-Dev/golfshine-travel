Crans-sur-Sierre Golf Club

Crans-Montana · Switzerland

Experience the epitome of luxury and golfing excellence with our exclusive package, featuring the prestigious Crans-sur-Sierre Golf Club, complemented by a lavish stay at the renowned Grand Hôtel du Golf et Palace.

Nestled amidst the stunning Swiss Alps, the Crans-sur-Sierre Golf Club is a testament to world-class golfing. With its challenging courses and breathtaking panoramic views, it has captured the hearts of golf enthusiasts worldwide. Tee off against the backdrop of majestic peaks, navigate fairways meticulously designed to challenge players of all levels, and putt on perfectly manicured greens.

After a day of exhilarating golf, retreat to the opulent Grand Hôtel du Golf et Palace, an icon of elegance and refined hospitality. Each room seamlessly blends classic charm with modern comforts, offering a haven of relaxation. Indulge in exceptional dining experiences at the hotel's restaurants, where gourmet cuisine tantalizes the palate.

For moments of pure indulgence, the hotel's spa presents a sanctuary of rejuvenation, pampering you with a range of treatments amidst breathtaking alpine views. The convenience of the hotel's location allows you to explore the enchanting town of Crans-Montana, offering a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural richness.

Our Crans-sur-Sierre Golf Club package invites you to a harmonious fusion of exceptional golfing and opulent living. Whether you're a passionate golfer seeking an unforgettable tee time or a discerning traveler in pursuit of luxury, this offering promises an unparalleled experience. Elevate your Swiss Alps escape with the allure of championship golf and lavish accommodation, crafting memories that will linger long after your departure.

Suggested hotel: Grand Hôtel du Golf et Palace
