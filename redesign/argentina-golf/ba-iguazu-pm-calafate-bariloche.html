Knowing Argentina - BA, Iguazú, Puerto Madryn, El Calafate & Bariloche

Argentina Golf · Multi-Region

KNOWING ARGENTINA - With this tour you will know the city of Buenos Aires, known as the most cosmopolitan city in Latin America. You will walk on the runways that into the forest and you will see the beautiful waterfalls of the Argentinean Falls. You will be thrilled to see the whales from Peninsula Valdez. You will stay frizz when contemplating the vastness of the Perito Moreno Glacier and spend great moments in Bariloche, knowing its Civic Center and gazing at any time Lake Nahuel Huapi.

The program includes the following services:

  • All destinations: Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Buenos Aires: 4-night accommodation. City Tour. Dinner & Tango Show. Golf Day.
  • Iguazú: 3-night accommodation. Argentinean & Brazilian falls.
  • Puerto Madryn: 3-night accommodation. Punta Tombo penguins, Peninsula Valdés whales.
  • El Calafate: 3-night accommodation. Perito Moreno Glacier bridges.
  • Bariloche: 3-night accommodation. Victoria Island & Arrayanes Forest. Golf Day.

A premium 5-region golf circuit covering the major regions of Argentina with Golf Days in Buenos Aires and Bariloche. Day-by-day plan follows the same pattern as the non-golf 16-night program.
