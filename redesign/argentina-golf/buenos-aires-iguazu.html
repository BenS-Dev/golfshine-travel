Buenos Aires & Iguazú

Argentina Golf · 7 Days / 6 Nights

The program includes the following services:

  • In Buenos Aires: 3-night lodging in the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Golf Day.
  • Transfers to Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Tour of Buenos Aires City.
  • In Iguazú: 3-night lodging in the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Falls from Argentine and Brazilian sides.
  • Dinner & Tango Show.

Day 1: Buenos Aires

Welcome to the Tango city, we were waiting for you! Once you arrive at Ezeiza International Airport, you will be taken to your chosen hotel. During the evening, a City Tour will take place. The rest of the day is free. Lodging.

Day 2: Buenos Aires - Golf Day

After breakfast, get ready for a golf day. Transfers Included. Lodging.

Day 3: Buenos Aires / Iguazú

Breakfast. Early in the morning you will take the flight to Iguazú. You will step on the typical red soil of Misiones and you can almost be splashed by the Falls. Transfer to the chosen hotel.

Day 4: Iguazú

Breakfast. During the morning you could visit the water falls, on the Argentinean side or on the Brazilian side, or both. As they are so marvelous, we suggest you visit only one side each day. Lodging.

Day 5: Iguazú

Breakfast. Free day but the adventure and the jungle are waiting for you. In The Great Adventure you could be as close as possible to the Garganta del Diablo, or maybe you could go along some of the paths, among typical flora, monkeys and jungle sounds.

Day 6: Iguazú / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. Transfered to the Iguazú Airport to take the flight back to Buenos Aires City. Once you arrive there, night Tango Show at Esquina Carlos Gardel. Accommodation in chosen hotel.

Day 7: Buenos Aires

Breakfast. According to your flight time, you could take advantage and take another activity or half-day excursion. Transfer from the hotel to Ezeiza International Airport.
