Bariloche & San Martin de los Andes

Argentina Golf · 6 Days / 5 Nights

A 6-day, 5-night combination golf program in the Argentine Andes lake district. Golf at Llao Llao or Arelauquen in Bariloche, then drive the Seven Lakes route to San Martín de los Andes for Chapelco Golf Club.

The program includes the following services:

  • In Bariloche: Lodging at the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Golf Day in Bariloche.
  • Circuito Chico and Cerro Catedral Excursion.
  • Seven Lakes Excursion.
  • In San Martín de los Andes: 2-night lodging at the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Golf Day in San Martín de los Andes.
  • Suggested Courses: Llao Llao, Arelauquen (Bariloche); Chapelco Golf Club (San Martín).

Day 1: Buenos Aires / Bariloche

In the Airport of Buenos Aires City, you will take a flight heading to The Andes Mountain Rage. When you arrive in Bariloche you could immediately see the Cathedral Mountain. Transfers to the chosen hotel. The rest of the day is free. According to your flight time you could have an excursion or carry out any other activity. Lodging.

Day 2: Bariloche — Golf Day

Breakfast. Golf Day. Transfers included. In the afternoon, return to hotel. Lodging.

Day 3: Bariloche

Breakfast. Today will be the perfect day for go on the Circuito Chico excursion. If not, you´ll have a free day: Victoria Island and the Arrayanes Forest. Sailing on the Nahuel Huapi Lake and a visit to this unique forest in the world. Lodging.

Day 4: Bariloche / San Martín de los Andes

Breakfast. Transfer to San Martín de los Andes on the route of "Seven Lakes". Transfer to the chosen hotel for your lodging.

Day 5: San Martín de los Andes — Golf Day

Breakfast. Golf Day. Transfers included. In the night, return to hotel. Lodging.

Day 6: San Martín de los Andes / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. Free day. Transfer to Bariloche City to go to the Aiport.
