Buenos Aires & El Calafate

Argentina Golf · 7 Days / 6 Nights

The program includes the following services:

  • In Buenos Aires: 3-night lodging in the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers to Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Tour of the City of Buenos Aires.
  • Golf Day.
  • In El Calafate: 3-night lodging at the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Traditional excursion to the Perito Moreno Glacier.
  • Dinner & Tango Show.

Day 1: Buenos Aires

Welcome to the Tango city, we were waiting for you! Once you arrive at Ezeiza International Airport, you will be taken to the chosen hotel. During the evening, a City Tour will take place. The rest of the day is free. Night at the hotel.

Day 2: Buenos Aires — Golf Day

After breakfast, get ready for a golf day. Transfers Included. At night, return to hotel. Accommodation.

Day 3: Buenos Aires / El Calafate

Breakfast. Transfer to Airport of Buenos Aires, to fly to the Patagonia. You will arrive in El Calafate, almost feeling the icy cold from the Glaciers. Transfer from the Airport to the chosen hotel. The Tour Calafate Balcony will give you an introduction to the adventure.

Day 4: El Calafate

First of all, your breakfast; because the Perito Moreno´s Glacier will still be there! Take warm clothes and a photo camera ready to take loads of pictures of your visit. Consult us on the Estancia Alice, where you could taste a typical Patagonian barbecue, or even have the continental ice caps so close to you, with a walk right upon the Perito Moreno Glacier. Lodging.

Day 5: El Calafate

Breakfast. Your day is completely free. In the Upsala Explorer excursion, you´ll be surrounded by ice floes, glaciers, patagonic winds and adventure without limit. It will be a day that you´ll never forget for the rest of your life. Lodging.

Day 6: El Calafate / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. Transfer to El Calafate airport. Arriving at Buenos Aires airport. Dinner Tango Show at Carlos Gardel Corner. Accommodation at the chosen hotel.

Day 7: Buenos Aires

Breakfast. According to your flight time, you could take advantage of your time to have a walk. Transfer from the hotel to Ezeiza International Airport.
