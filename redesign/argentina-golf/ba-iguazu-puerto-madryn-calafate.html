Argentina Adventure - BA, Iguazú, Puerto Madryn & El Calafate

Argentina Golf · 15 Days / 13 Nights

ARGENTINA ADVENTURE - This time, you will enjoy the city of Buenos Aires and you will know the secrets of the tango. Soak up the excitement and beauty, with waterfalls of Iguazú Falls. You will be able to be on feet of the Southern Right Whale in Puerto Madryn and finally you will stay frozen when contemplating the incredible Perito Moreno Glacier in El Calafate.

The program includes the following services:

  • All destinations: Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Buenos Aires: 4-night accommodation. City Tour. Dinner & Tango Show. Golf Day.
  • Iguazú: 3-night accommodation. Argentinean & Brazilian falls.
  • Puerto Madryn: 3-night accommodation. Punta Tombo penguins, Peninsula Valdés whales.
  • El Calafate: 3-night accommodation. Perito Moreno Glacier bridges.

A 15-day / 13-night golf variant adding a Golf Day in Buenos Aires to the four-region whales-and-glaciers loop. Day-by-day plan follows the non-golf 13-night program.
