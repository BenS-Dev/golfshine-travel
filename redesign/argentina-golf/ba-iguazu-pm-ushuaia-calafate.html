Full Patagonia — BA, Iguazú, Puerto Madryn, Ushuaia & El Calafate

Argentina Golf · 18 Days / 16 Nights

FULL PATAGONIA — Ciudad de Buenos Aires, Iguazú, Puerto Madryn, Ushuaia, El Calafate. 18 days / 16 nights with two golf days in Buenos Aires and a golf day in Ushuaia.

The program includes the following services:

  • All destinations: Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Buenos Aires: 4-night lodging. City Tour. Dinner & Tango Show. Two golf days.
  • Iguazú: 3-night lodging. Argentinean & Brazilian falls.
  • Puerto Madryn: 3-night lodging. Punta Tombo penguins, Peninsula Valdés.
  • Ushuaia: 3-night lodging. Lago Fagnano off road 4X4. Golf day.
  • El Calafate: 3-night lodging. Perito Moreno Glacier passages.
  • Not Included: Domestic or International flights · Entrances to National Park.

A premium 18-day / 16-night five-region golf circuit. Day-by-day plan follows the non-golf 16-night program with golf days added in Buenos Aires (Day 3) and Ushuaia.
