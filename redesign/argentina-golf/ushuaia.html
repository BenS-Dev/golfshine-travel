Ushuaia Golf - 4 Days / 3 Nights

Argentina Golf

Ushuaia is the capital of the Province of Tierra del Fuego, Antarctica and South Atlantic Islands. It was founded on October 12, 1884 by Augusto Lasserre and is located on the shores of the Beagle Channel surrounded by the Martial mountain range, in the Bay of Ushuaia. Besides being an administrative center, it is an industrial, port and tourist knot. Ushuaia is known as the southernmost city in the world.

The program includes the following services:

  • Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • 3 nights lodging with breakfast.
  • Excursion: Tierra del Fuego National Park + Train to The End of the World.
  • Golf Day.

Day 1: Buenos Aires / Ushuaia

Transfer to the airport of Buenos Aires. You will head for "The End of the World" and will arrive in the southernmost city that you ever visit. Transfer to the selected hotel. The rest of the day is free. Night at the hotel.

Day 2: Ushuaia

Breakfast and departure for the Tierra del Fuego National Park + Train to the End of the World. There you could see nature at its best. And if you want to be more adventurous you could enjoy the park the whole day. And don´t get desperate, tomorrow you will have many things to do. Lodging.

Day 3: Ushuaia - Golf Day

Breakfast. Golf Day. Transfers included. At night, return to hotel. Lodging.

Day 4: Ushuaia / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. Transfer to Ushuaia airport and we assure you that someday you will return here. Arriving at Buenos Aires airport. At night, Tango Show at Esquina Carlos Gardel. At the appointed time, transfer to the airport in Buenos Aires.

Golf Day & Car Rental in Ushuaia
