Cordoba Golf — 4 Days / 3 Nights

Argentina Golf

The province of Cordoba amazes with its many tourist attractions, framed by various mountain systems of western and northwestern provincial. Its rivers and streams, endless green, wooded areas, the mild climate and unique natural landscapes attract visitors all year towards this privileged world. Córdoba has stunning walks through the various systems of saws. Dams and reservoirs, natural, historical and cultural attractions, to which are added a prodigious climate make the province of Cordoba one of the most significant tourist level.

The program includes the following services:

  • In Córdoba: Accommodation in the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • City Tour.
  • Golf Day.

Day 1: Buenos Aires / Cordoba

Arrival. We suggest you to visit Córdoba´s Historical Center and get dazzled by the most valuable cultural heritage of the province. Lodging.

Day 2: Cordoba — Golf Day

Breakfast. Golf Day. Transfers Included. At night, return to hotel. Lodging.

Day 3: Cordoba, Villa General Belgrano y La Cumbrecita (Free Day)

Breakfast. City Tour: Departing from Downtown Córdoba, you will enjoy a city tour, during which you will discover the strong contrasts that are typical of this city and surprise every visitor. Round of golf in the afternoon. Return to the hotel. Lodging.

Day 4: Cordoba / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. We are pleased to receive you again in Buenos Aires.

Golf Day & Car Rental in Cordoba
