From North to South - BA, Iguazú, Bariloche & El Calafate

Argentina Golf · 15 Days / 13 Nights

FROM NORTH TO SOUTH - On this trip, you will know the charm of the city of Buenos Aires and tango as well known. The incredible jumps of Iguazú Falls, the beauty of Bariloche and Lake Nahuel Huapi. In El Calafate.

The program includes the following services:

  • All destinations: Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Buenos Aires: 4-night accommodation in the chosen hotel with breakfast. A Tour along Buenos Aires City. Dinner & Tango Show. Golf Day.
  • Iguazú: 3-night accommodation. Excursion to Iguazú Falls on Argentinean and Brazilian sides.
  • Bariloche: 3-night accommodation. Circuito Chico excursion, Victoria Island & Arrayanes Forest. Golf Day.
  • El Calafate: 3-night accommodation. Excursion to Perito Moreno Glacier passages.

A 15-day / 13-night golf variant of the Argentine northern-to-southern circuit. Golf rounds in Buenos Aires and Bariloche, paired with the falls, Andean lakes, and Patagonian glaciers. The day-by-day plan mirrors the non-golf 13-night program with added Golf Days at Buenos Aires and Bariloche.
