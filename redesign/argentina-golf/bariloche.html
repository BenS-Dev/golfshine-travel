Bariloche Golf — 4 Days / 3 Nights

Argentina Golf

Bariloche is the most visited city in Patagonia and third nationally tourist destination. It is the most populous city in the Argentina Patagonian Andes. It is located in the Nahuel Huapi National Park, in the southwestern province of Rio Negro, near the Andes, on the southern shore of Lake Nahuel Huapi. Its natural reserves, including lakes, forests and mountains, and their outstanding ski resorts are the main attraction and economic livelihood of this city. The city has been considered by some sources as the second ski destination worldwide.

The program includes the following services:

  • In Bariloche: Accommodation at the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Circuito Chico Excursion. (a short tour of the nearby areas)
  • Golf Day.

Day 1: Buenos Aires / Bariloche

In the Buenos Aires City Airport, you will take a flight heading for Los Andes Mountain Rage. When you arrive in Bariloche you could immediately see Cathedral Mountain. Transfers to the chosen hotel. The rest of the day is free. According to your flight time you could take an excursion or any other activity.

Day 2: Bariloche — Golf Day

Breakfast. Golf Day. Transfers included. At night, return to hotel. Lodging.

Day 3: Bariloche

Breakfast. Free day, we have thought up something special for you: Victoria Island and the Arrayanes Forest. You can´t miss it!: sailing on the deep blue waters of the Nahuel Huapi Lake and visiting this unique forest in the world. Or also sailing to Puerto Blest and Los Cántaros Cascade.

Day 4: Bariloche / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. Transfer to the Airport of San Carlos de Bariloche, with your hands full of chocolates as it almost always happens. Arrival at the Aiport of Buenos Aires City. Transfer to the chosen hotel.
