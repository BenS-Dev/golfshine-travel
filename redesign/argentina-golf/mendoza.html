Mendoza Golf - 4 Days / 3 Nights

Argentina Golf · Wine Country

Mendoza offers diverse Good Living experiences focused on its internationally acknowledged wines and excellent gastronomy, the privilege of a definitely beautiful landscape and remarkable quality services. In this context, golf makes its contribution with fields located at exclusive environments, close to the most visited tourist destinations.

The program includes the following services:

  • In Mendoza: Accommodation in the chosen hotel with breakfast.
  • Transfers to Airport / Hotel / Airport.
  • Golf Day.
  • Winery Tour.

Not included: Domestic or international flights · Entrances to National Parks.

Day 1: Buenos Aires / Mendoza

In the Airport of Buenos Aires City you will take the flight to Mendoza. Transfer to the chosen hotel. According to your flight time you can carry any leisure activity or touristic excursion.

Day 2: Mendoza - Golf Day

After breakfast, we will prepare for a golf's day. Transfers included. At night, return to hotel. Lodging.

Day 3: Mendoza - Winery

Breakfast. Visit to a Wine Cellar: Wine tours have become a classic in Mendoza. Both local and foreign tourists come along to be delighted by the flavors and fragrances of this beverage. Wineries open their gates and unveil their secrets.

Day 4: Mendoza / Buenos Aires

Breakfast. Transfer to Mendoza Airport to return to Buenos Aires. Arrival in Buenos Aires City. Arriva at the airport. We are pleased to receive you again.

Golf Day & Car Rental in Mendoza

The program includes the following services: One day Car Rental of a Compact Car with 200 km. Allowance. Car Insurance Franchise included. Green fee 18 holes. Caddie included. Gratuities not included.

  • Single: u$s 251.-
  • Double: u$s 178.-

Price per person expressed in current U.S Dollars, in double room, with taxes included. Except for winter holidays, public holidays or congress days. Net prices include the detailed services in each tour. Domestic flights are not included.
